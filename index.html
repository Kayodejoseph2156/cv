We also process the fresh Catfish into smokedcatfish thereby increasing the shelf-life of the fish without loosing it's nutritional value. We are focusing on live stock farming which will include aquaculture, animal husbandry and poultry. We have being producing catfish and chicken since 2008 and will be starting the production of tilapia and lobster in 2021.

About Our Company:

We are a fast growing Poultry Layer operation. We have been in the egg production business for the past 12years and we enjoy the reputation in the market place as providers of products with consistent quality & good scientific management practices. Our facilities include a start of the art Microbiology & feed testing lab for continuous improvement & Development. We are an vertically integrated operation with own feed mill & Chick and grower operations. We have our own Retailing set up also. Our farm is a modern farm with all the birds in Cages in elevated open sided sheds with automatic feeding & egg collection. We are into manufacture of Table eggs & Layer mash feed for domestic market & exports. We have capability to produce clean eggs (For Vaccine production) & can supply Pullet birds at point of lay.
